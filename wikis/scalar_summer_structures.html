Items

Rose Gold Alloy Compound
Rose Gold Alloy Compound is essential in making Rose Gold Ingots.
They can be crafted shapelessly from 1 Gold Ingot and 1 Copper Ingot.
Rose Gold Ingot
Rose Gold Ingots can be used as an alternative to Gold Ingots in most recipes.
By smelting 1 Rose Gold Alloy Compound in a blast furnace, you can obtain 1 Rose Gold Ingot.
Rose Gold's primary use is to make Rose Gold Tools, which have the speed of Gold Tools, but a higher durability.
Note: These were initially intended to give Copper more uses, before 1.21.8 was announced.
Rose Golden Axe
