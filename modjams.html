What is a Mod Jam?

A Mod Jam is a short, time-limited event where mod authors create small projects, prototypes, or themed mods. These builds are often experimental and focus on creativity and rapid iteration rather than long-term support. Below are a few Mod Jam projects and community mods we've released.
