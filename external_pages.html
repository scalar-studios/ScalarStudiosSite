Licenses

We utilize a variety of Open Source licenses for our projects:
GNU General Public License v3
LemonDev FOSS License 1.x

We like GPLv3 because it ensures that our code, and any modifications to it, remain open and accessible, preventing organizations from taking it private or locking us out of our own work.
It offers strong legal protections, including safeguards against patent claims, and is written clearly with global use in mind.
For a small team, it helps us retain control over our software while encouraging open collaboration.

Additionally, we have our own custom license, developed by Lemon Juice Dev, which is used for some of our projects.
The LemonDev FOSS License v1.1a is our own lightweight, permissive license designed to support open collaboration without adding legal complexity.
